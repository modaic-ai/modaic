title: Use Modaic Arbiters in Braintrust
sidebarTitle: Braintrust

You can use an Arbiter from Modaic Hub as an scorer in Braintrust. This allows you to score Braintrust traces with an aligned continously improving evaluator.

1. Create your arbiter on Modaic Hub

2. Go to your Braintrust settings under the "Settings" tab at the bottom of the side menu.
[]

3. Navigate to "Env variables" on the Settings menu and add your `MODAIC_TOKEN` as an environment variable.

4.

You cannot use arbiters through the UI. You must upload using CLI or Braintrust SDK.
</Warning> */}
